Research Interests

Number theory and arithmetic algebraic geometry.


About me

I am a tenure-tracked researcher at Zhejiang University. I got my Ph.D. at University of Maryland, College Park in 2019 under the supervision of John Millson, and co-advised by Michael Rapoport. I was a Van Vleck Visiting Assitant Professor at University of Wisconsin, Madison, from August 2019 to May 2020, and from August 2021 to May 2023. (My CV)
